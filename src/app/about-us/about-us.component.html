About US

WELCOME TO SHONIE HOTELS & RESORTS.

Since 2018 Shonies Hotel has built a fine reputation for offering luxury hotels
and conference venues carefully situated in the mainstream of regional business
and travel destinations in South Africa.

Shonies Hotel Hotels & Resorts was established when current Managing Director
Sam Nassimov purchased an old hotel in East London in the Eastern Cape, with
a view to turning it into a boutique establishment. Offering discerning clients
a home away from home, the first branded ShoniesHotel was born.

From a humble 40 bedrooms, Premier Hotels & Resorts’ portfolio has grown to
encompass 16 hotels and resorts throughout the country, offering nearly
2000 bedrooms and providing employment opportunities
to over 1500 passionate members of staff.

As an award-winning hospitality company with more than 25 years’ experience
developing and managing hotels, conference centres, boutique concept hotels and
restaurants, Shonies Hotels & Resorts has a proven reputation for delivering superior
results through forward focused ingenuity and exceptional asset management.

Shonies Hotels & Resorts’ is set to continue its national expansion into hotel
property development and management, providing operational expertise to steer
hospitality ventures and developing partnerships that enable consumers
to experience superior guest services.


Our Hotel

3 Stars Shonies Hotel


Situated in the heart of Limpopo Vhembe district business district and rated one of
the best hotels in the Polokwane, the three star, stylish Shonies Hotel is centrally
located – ideal for the overnight traveler or business executive.

Shonies Hotel Bloemfontein Central is a three star hotel, offering four star
services and facilities, and is conveniently located in the Makhado Crossing
Plaza shopping centre, close to all transport nodes, businesses and tourist attractions
. The hotel offers a complimentary shuttle service to all guests around Bloemfontein
and to and from Bloemfontein Airport which is just 10 kllometres away.

Our hotel has 115 spacious and comfortable rooms, each with en-suite bathrooms and
complimentray wi-fi. Make your choice from a selection of Suites and Standard Rooms.

Our 6 conference rooms can accommodate up to 420 delegates and provide modern,
open spaces for gala dinners, themed events, product launches, trade shows,
workshops and weddings. There is also a 24 hour business centre to cater
to all your business needs.

The Courtroom Restaurant is open daily for breakfast and dinner and offers a
delicious carvery buffet. There is a grab-and-go breakfast option for the
early riser too. Alternatively you can relax at the Judges Bar with colleagues
and associates or grab a quick lunch in between meetings. At the end of a
busy day you can enjoy your favourite cocktail at the bar.

For the gym fanatics there is a fitness centre 500 metres from the hotel, an
indoor heated swimming pool, tennis and squash courts and a golf course.
The more adventurous traveller can horse ride, water ski, hike, or take
in a scenic city or game reserve tour.

Whether travelling for business or pleasure, Shonies Hotel Polokwane
Central provides a good base to work from.
